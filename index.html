Our Philosophy

The Icon of Modernity

ICON DESIGN HOMES LTDA, operating as ONE HAUS CASAS CONTEMPORANEAS, represents the new era of civil construction in Santa Catarina. Led by Maria Eugenia Simas Zucki, our firm merges the technical rigor of non-residential engineering with the refined aesthetics of luxury living.

Located in Balneário Camboriú, a city known for its towering skyline and demanding standards, we specialize in delivering high-performance buildings. Whether it is a bespoke contemporary residence or a complex commercial structure, our approach remains constant: form must serve function, and construction must endure.

We are not just builders; we are curators of space. We understand that in the high-end market, every detail counts—from the structural foundation to the final play of light in a living room. One Haus is where engineering meets art.
